Instructions: Non-Technical Users

For non-technical users, creating your own sign language translator is as simple as

  1. zip your collection of images segregated by class
  2. post it and provide the classes of the images
  3. your set to go!!!
Code for Windows(powershell)
Compress-Archive -Path 'directory' -DestinationPath 'archive.zip'

Code for Linux/Mac-os
zip -rq9 archive.zip 'file-path'
